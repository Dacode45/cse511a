## Deterministic games
Many possible formulations, on is
- States: S (start at s0)
- Players: P={1..N} (usually take turns)
- Actions: A (may be depend on player/state)
- Transition Function: SxA -> S
- Terminal Test: S -> {t, f}

## Zero-Sum game
